An all-time favorite activity in our local church club is "skrammel-aften" - trash night.

An activity that I came up with, inspired by a summer when, as a kid, I was allowed to tear down an old television with my dad and my brother.

I collect old non-functional electronic equipment like laptops, keyboards, speakers etc. and then I hand the kids screwdrivers and let them tear everything apart. I deliberately omit hammers and encourage curiosity while "trashing".

As soon as the black plastic shells are removed, the kids start asking what the different components do... always a winner, except for the hardware that is...
